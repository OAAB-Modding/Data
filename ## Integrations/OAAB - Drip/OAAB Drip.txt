OAAB Drip

This mod adds OAAB_Data items to the Drip loot tables.

Drip adds Diablo 2 style loot to Morrowind. Unique weapons, armor, clothing and accessories are dynamically generated, with over a hundred unique modifiers and more than a million possible combinations.
